Dr. Chitresh Kumar Sharma

M.Ch. Endocrine Surgery

About Me

Hi, I am Dr. Chitresh Kumar Sharma (M.B.B.S., M.S., M.Ch., F.MAS., F.A.C.S.) currently working as Associate Professor Surgical Oncology at AIIMS Bilaspur, Himachal Pradesh. Previously served at National Cancer Institute, AIIMS, Jhajjar as Assistant Professor.

Areas of specialisation & key skills

  • Thyroid: Thyroid Nodules, MNG, Retrosternal Goitre, Graves’ disease, Thyroid Cancers, Endoscopic Thyroidectomy
  • Parathyroid: Hyperparathyroidism, Parathyroid Cancer, Endoscopic Parathyroidectomy.
  • Adrenal: Myelolipoma, Pheochromocytoma, Cushing’s & Conn’s Adenoma, Adrenal Cancer, Laparoscopic & Retroperitoneoscopic Adrenalectomy
  • Pancreas: Insulinoma, Gastrinoma, Glucagonoma, VIPomas and others
  • Breast: Conservative Breast Surgeries, Oncoplastic procedures, Mastectomies, Sentinel Node biopsy, and TIVAD insertion
  • Neuroendocrine tumors: Carcinoids, Paragangliomas, and Carotid Body Tumor
